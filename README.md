## Evaluating Your Free Kratom Samples

Receiving samples represents only the beginning; proper evaluation ensures informed purchasing decisions.

### Quality Assessment Criteria

Examine samples using these objective quality indicators:

**Visual Inspection**: High-quality kratom powder displays consistent color matching the vein type (green, red, white, or yellow), fine texture without excessive stems or debris, and uniform particle size.

**Aroma Evaluation**: Fresh kratom emits an earthy, slightly bitter, tea-like scent. Musty, moldy, or chemical odors indicate poor storage or contamination.

**Texture Analysis**: Powder should feel smooth and fine between fingers, not gritty, clumpy, or excessively dry. Proper moisture content prevents both clumping and dustiness.

**Taste Testing**: While kratom's natural bitterness is unavoidable, quality samples shouldn't taste excessively harsh, chemical, or unpleasant beyond the typical bitter profile.

**Dissolution Test**: When mixed with liquid, quality kratom powder should disperse relatively evenly without excessive clumping or floating particles.
